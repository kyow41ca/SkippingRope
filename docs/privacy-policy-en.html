Privacy Policy

This policy describes how the Apple Watch app "Jump Rope" (the "App") handles personal information.

Data We Collect

The App stores the following data on your device:

  • Workout records (date, duration, jump count, calories burned)

HealthKit Usage

The App uses HealthKit to record workouts and calculate calories burned. Data obtained from HealthKit is used solely to provide app functionality and is never shared with third parties or used for advertising purposes.

Data Storage

All data is stored exclusively on your Apple Watch. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Tracking

The App does not perform any advertising tracking.
